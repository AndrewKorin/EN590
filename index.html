Who We Are

BUILT FROM
THE GROUND UP

Our company's story begins not in a trading room — but deep underground. For over 25 years, we were at the forefront of mineral exploration and extraction, working directly with geologists, mining engineers, and production teams across multiple continents.

"That direct field experience gave us something most traders don't have — a profound understanding of how commodities are born: from exploration and drilling to production, refining, and logistics."

This immersion in the full supply chain — exploration, extraction, production, transportation — gave us an unmatched understanding of the real economics behind every barrel, every metric ton, every shipment.

15 years ago, we leveraged that expertise to enter global commodity trading. Our clients gained a partner who doesn't just quote prices — but understands quality parameters, logistics constraints, customs requirements, and the technical realities at both ends of a transaction.

Based in New York, USA, we operate globally with discretion. Company documentation and full credentials are available strictly upon request to verified counterparties.

What We Trade

OUR PRODUCT RANGE

From crude oil and refined petroleum products to industrial sulphur and agricultural fertilizers — we source, qualify, and deliver globally.

Diesel Fuel

EN590
DIESEL

European standard ultra-low sulphur diesel. The gold standard for commercial and industrial applications worldwide.

  • Sulphur content: max 10 ppm
  • Standard: EN 590:2022
  • Flash point: min 55°C
  • Cetane number: min 51
  • Origin: EU, Baltic, Black Sea
  • Delivery: CIF, FOB, DAP available
Crude Oil

CRUDE OIL
BLENDS

Multiple grades sourced from established producing regions. Urals, CPC Blend, ESPO, and spot availability on request.

  • Grades: Urals, CPC, ESPO, Basrah
  • API gravity: 28–42°
  • Sulphur: sweet & sour grades
  • Vessel sizes: Aframax to VLCC
  • Inspection: SGS / Bureau Veritas
Aviation

JET FUEL
JET A-1

Aviation kerosene meeting DEF STAN 91-091 and ASTM D1655 specifications. Available for international hubs.

  • Spec: DEF STAN 91-091 / ASTM D1655
  • Freeze point: max −47°C
  • Flash point: min 38°C
  • Sulphur: max 0.30% m/m
  • Additives: antistatic, antioxidant
Fuel Oil

MAZUT
M100

Heavy residual fuel oil used in power generation, heating, and marine applications. Available in large volumes.

  • Viscosity: 50–380 cSt at 50°C
  • Sulphur: 0.5% – 3.5% grades
  • Flash point: min 80°C
  • Ash content: max 0.14%
  • Minimum order: 10,000 MT
Bitumen

BITUMEN
60/70 · 80/100

Road-grade bitumen for infrastructure projects. Paving grade and modified bitumen available for major construction.

  • Grades: 60/70, 80/100, VG-30, VG-40
  • Penetration: as per grade
  • Softening point: 46–54°C
  • Packaging: drums, bulk tankers
  • Origin: Iran, Russia, Gulf
Gasoline

GASOLINE
92 · 95 · 98

Unleaded motor gasoline in multiple octane grades. Compliant with European and international fuel standards.

  • RON: 92, 95, 98
  • Lead content: max 0.005 g/L
  • Benzene: max 1.0% v/v (EU)
  • RVP: seasonal specification
  • Standard: EN 228
Natural Gas

NATURAL GAS
LNG · CNG

Pipeline natural gas and liquefied natural gas (LNG) for industrial, power generation, and municipal supply applications.

  • Methane content: min 85%
  • Calorific value: 38–42 MJ/m³
  • H2S: max 5 mg/m³
  • LNG: −162°C storage
  • Origin: Russia, Qatar, Algeria, USA
LPG

LPG
PROPANE · BUTANE

Liquefied petroleum gas for residential heating, industrial processing, and petrochemical feedstock applications.

  • Propane content: min 95%
  • Butane content: commercial mix
  • Moisture: max 50 mg/kg
  • Packaging: ISO tanks, bulk vessels
  • Standard: ASTM D1835
Petroleum Gas

PETROLEUM
NAPHTHA

Light and heavy naphtha for petrochemical cracking, gasoline blending, and chemical production feedstocks.

  • Light naphtha: IBP 30–80°C
  • Heavy naphtha: IBP 80–180°C
  • Sulphur: max 100 ppm
  • Applications: reforming, cracking
Granulated

SULPHUR
GRANULATED

Free-flowing granulated sulphur ideal for fertilizer production, chemical manufacturing, and rubber vulcanization.

  • Purity: min 99.5%
  • Moisture: max 0.5%
  • Acidity: max 0.003% as H₂SO₄
  • Granule size: 1–6 mm (98% min)
  • Ash: max 0.03%
  • Origin: Middle East, Russia, Kazakhstan
Lump / Crushed

SULPHUR
LUMP

Coarse crushed sulphur for industrial chemical processes, metallurgical applications, and sulphuric acid production.

  • Purity: min 99.5%
  • Moisture: max 1.0%
  • Lump size: 25–150 mm
  • Application: H₂SO₄ production
  • Packaging: bulk vessel / big bags
Liquid

LIQUID
SULPHUR

Molten elemental sulphur transported in heated tankers or rail cars for direct industrial use and production processes.

  • Purity: min 99.5%
  • Temperature: 125–160°C
  • H₂S content: max 10 ppm
  • Transport: heated tankers
  • Application: fertilizers, chemicals
Nitrogen

UREA 46%
GRANULATED

High-analysis nitrogen fertilizer. Granulated urea with 46% N content — the most concentrated solid nitrogen source available.

  • Nitrogen: min 46.0% (as N)
  • Moisture: max 0.5%
  • Biuret: max 1.0%
  • Granule size: 2–4 mm (90% min)
  • Origin: Russia, Middle East, China
  • Standard: ISO 2602
Nitrogen

UREA 46%
PRILLED

Prilled urea for direct application and industrial use. Suitable for industrial processes requiring fast dissolution.

  • Nitrogen: min 46.0% (as N)
  • Moisture: max 0.5%
  • Biuret: max 1.0%
  • Prill size: 1–2.5 mm (95% min)
  • Applications: agriculture, industrial
Complex NPK

NPK
FERTILIZERS

Complex NPK fertilizers in multiple grades for various crop requirements and soil conditions across global markets.

  • Grades: 16-16-16, 15-15-15, 20-20-20
  • Nitrogen (N): 15–20%
  • Phosphate (P₂O₅): 15–20%
  • Potash (K₂O): 15–20%
  • Custom grades on request
  • Packaging: 50kg bags, big bags, bulk
